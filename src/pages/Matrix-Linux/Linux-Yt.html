Usefull Commands That I Found.

  • For Downloading Seperate Chapters In A Video:

    
yt-dlp --embed-metadata --split-chapters $URL
OR:
yt-dlp --embed-chapters --split-chapters $URL

  • Best Download Speed(Needs aria2 and yt-dlp maybe):

    
youtube-dl --external-downloader=aria2c --external-downloader-args '--min-split-size=1M --max-connection-per-server=16 --max-concurrent-downloads=16 --split=16' $URL

  • For Extracting audio.:

    
youtube-dl --extract-audio --audio-format mp3 $URL

  • For Adding Thimbnail and Metadata.:

    
yt-dlp --extract-audio --audio-format best --embed-thumbnail --add-metadata $URL

  • For extracting actual video url. The first one is the Video while the second one is the audio.:

    
youtube-dl -g

  • Using ffmpeg to cut the video.:

    
ffmpeg -ss 08:51 -to 69:24 -i "(URL)" -ss 08:51 -to 69:24 -i "(URL) -c copy -avoid_negative_ts make_zero nameofthefile.mp4

  • For Downloading Thumbnail.:

    
youtube-dl --write-thumbnail --skip-download $URL

  • For Mearging Audio and Video:

    
ffmpeg -i (Video Name).mp4 -i (Audio Name).mp3 -c:v -c:a copy output.mp4

  • Convert .webm into .mp4:

    
ffmpeg -i 1.webm -vf "pad=ceil(iw/2)*2:ceil(ih/2)*2" dsa.mp4

  • Write Subtitles:

    
--write-subs
